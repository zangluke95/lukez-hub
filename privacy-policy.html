Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026  ·  lukez.edu.kg

Plain English summary: This educational site doesn't collect personal information. We use Google AdSense for ads (which uses cookies) and Cloudflare for hosting. No sign-ups, no tracking of your personal data.

1. About This Site

This Privacy Policy applies to lukez.edu.kg, an educational resource website providing free tools and learning materials for students and learners, created and maintained by Luke Zang.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. Specifically, this site does not:

  • Require user registration or login
  • Collect names, emails, or contact details through automatic tracking
  • Store any user data on our servers
  • Use first-party analytics beyond what Cloudflare provides

If you use our Contact form, any message you send is delivered directly to us and is not stored in a database.

3. Advertising & Cookies

This site uses Google AdSense (Publisher ID: ca-pub-7966932624845484) to display relevant advertisements. Google uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Display ads based on your prior visits to this and other sites
  • Measure ad performance and engagement

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

4. Hosting & Infrastructure

This site is hosted via Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare acts as our CDN and may log standard web server data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) for security and performance optimization. Review Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts CDN, which may log your IP as part of the font delivery request.

5. Educational Content Disclaimer

Content on this site — including calculators, guides, and tools — is provided for educational and informational purposes only. It is not professional financial, legal, or academic advice.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

This site does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will remove it immediately.

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Because we do not collect or store personal data, most data rights requests should be directed to our third-party providers:

  • Google (Ads & Fonts): Google Account Settings
  • Cloudflare: Cloudflare Privacy

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy as our site evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most current version.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please use our Contact page.
