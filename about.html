About the Creator

Hi, I'm Luke Zang — a Finance student at the University of Florida and aspiring computer scientist with a background in AI, web development, and automation. I grew up in Millburn, New Jersey and have always believed that good tools and clear explanations can make any subject approachable.

This site started as a personal project: I wanted to build the best free financial calculators on the internet — tools I personally wished existed when I was learning about compound interest, loans, and personal finance in school.

What You'll Find Here

Financial Calculators

Compound interest, loan payoff, savings goals, and more — with visual charts and real-time results.
